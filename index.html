1. River severn (Afon Hafren)

🐑
  • Longest river in Britain 354km miles long (220 miles)
  • Average flow rate of 107 m³/s at Apperley, Gloucestershire
  • Flows through several counties:
    • Powys
    • Shropshire
    • Worcestershire
    • Gloucestershire
  • Ends in Bristol channel
